Privacy & GDPR — Information

This website is part of a student project. The following statements explain how personal data is (or is not) processed on this site.

Key statements

  • Student project: This site is created as a student/educational project and is not a commercial service.
  • No personal data collected or stored: The site does not collect or store personal data (names, emails, IP addresses) on any server controlled by the project authors.
  • Contact form data: Contact form submissions are not retained by the project. See details below.
  • Cookies & tracking: The site does not use analytics, third‑party trackers or non‑essential cookies. Only essential cookies required by the browser/Bootstrap may be used.

Contact form

The contact form on the landing page is implemented for demonstration. By default:

  • The form does not send data to a database or keep any copies on the project server.
  • If you (the site owner) later add server-side handling, ensure that incoming messages are either not stored or are deleted as soon as they are processed — and document retention periods.
  • If you prefer to receive messages via email client, consider switching the form to use mailto: or a known service with a clear privacy policy; update this page accordingly.

Legal basis & rights

As this is an educational/student project, processing is minimal. If any personal data processing is introduced later, update this page with: legal basis, retention periods, contact point for data subject requests, and how to exercise rights (access, rectification, erasure).
